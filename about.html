About Loft City

Welcome to Urban Holiday Apartments, where city life meets the comfort of home! Nestled in vibrant urban settings, our apartments offer a unique blend of modern design and cozy ambiance, perfect for both relaxation and adventure. Each unit is thoughtfully equipped with all the amenities you need for a memorable stay, from fully-equipped kitchens to high-speed WiFi. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our prime locations put you just steps away from the city's best attractions, dining, and entertainment. Experience the perfect urban getaway with us, where every stay is tailored to your needs!

Bronte Hotel

Enjoy international cuisine at Emmanuels, one of the hotel's 2 restaurants, or stay in and take advantage of the room service (during limited hours). Wrap up your day with a drink at the bar/lounge. A complimentary buffet breakfast is included. .

Cresta Oasis Hotel

Enjoy recreation amenities such as an outdoor pool or take in the view from a garden. This hotel also features complimentary wireless internet access, concierge services, and a television in a common area. Enjoy African cuisine at Cafe Afrique, a restaurant which features a bar/lounge, or stay in and take advantage of the room service. English breakfasts are available for a fee.

N1 Hotel Samora Machel Harare

With a stay at N1 Hotel Samora Machel Harare in Harare, you'll be within a 10-minute walk of National Gallery of Zimbabwe and National Art Gallery. This hotel is 0.6 mi (0.9 km) from Harare Gardens and 0.6 mi (1 km) from African Unity Square. Make use of convenient amenities, which include complimentary wireless internet access and a vending machine. At N1 Hotel Samora Machel Harare, enjoy a satisfying meal at the restaurant. Full breakfasts are available daily from 6:30 AM to 10 AM for a fee.
